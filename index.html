Methodology & trust

Built to feel credible because it is structured like a research library.

Every page foregrounds scope, evidence type, and editorial framing so readers can tell whether they’re looking at mechanism, synthesis, historical reporting, or open debate.

Evidence labels

Clear taxonomy

Pages separate foundational background, mechanistic argument, clinical relevance, historical context, and disputed interpretation.

Editorial process

Context before conclusions

Summaries are written to surface where evidence is strongest, where debate remains active, and which claims depend on extrapolation.

Design principle

Readable density

Typography, whitespace, and subtle cards carry the experience so the site feels premium and serious without resorting to wellness-brand styling.
